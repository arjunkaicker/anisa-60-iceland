Travel Information

Everything you need to know for the trip

✈

Getting There

Fly into Keflavik International Airport (KEF). Private transfers will be arranged from the airport to the hotel — approximately 90 minutes.

If arriving at different times, smaller vans will be grouped for transfers.

🏠

Accommodation

Nights 1 & 2: Black Sand Hotel Classic Rooms & Connecting Family Rooms
Nights 3 & 4: Blue Lagoon Retreat Moss Junior Suites & Family Suite
🚌

Transportation

Private coach seating up to 29 travellers provided for all touring days, along with a sit-in private guide.

🍴

Meals

Breakfast included daily with your accommodation. A private lunch at Friðheimar is arranged on Day 2.

Other lunches and dinners are on your own — recommendations will be provided!

💼

What to Pack

  • Warm layers & waterproof jacket
  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Swimsuit (for Blue Lagoon!)
  • Camera
  • Dressy outfit for celebration dinner
⚡

Good to Know

  • Iceland uses Icelandic Króna (ISK)
  • Cards accepted almost everywhere
  • No tipping culture in Iceland
  • Late July: ~20 hours of daylight!
  • Average temp: 10–15°C (50–59°F)
